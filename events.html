Events

Main Event, FTKolor Run, FTKids on the Block, Miracle Family Events

Events Summary

Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon hosts numerous events throughout the year to support local Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals. SLUDM has three primary events. Our FTKolor Run which is a 5k held in the spring used to kickstart our fundraising for the year, FTKids on the Block which is an event held to give students an opportunity to interact with our Miracle Families in a fun and family friendly manner, and our Main Event which is a 12 hour long Dance Marathon. We also host numerous small events such as a car wash, game and craft nights at the hospital and more!

Main Event

Our Main Event is a twelve hour long Celebration! During the event we celebrate a year’s worth of hard work, time, commitment, passion, dancing and fundraising done by those involved in our organization. We also celebrate the generosity of our donors and sponsors. Finally, we celebrate our Miracle Kiddos and their families, all of the birthdays, treatments, smiles, therapy sessions and obstacles they have overcome.

FTKolor Run

The FTKolor Run is a great way to start up our SLUDM 2019 year. This is going to be a color and joy filled 5K that anyone in the Saint Louis community can participate in! As always this is an event is going to bring funds, and hope to the kids and families at SSM Health Cardinal Glennon and St Louis Children’s Hospital!

FTKids on the Block

Introduced in 2018, FTKids on the Block gives students, Miracle Makers and other SLUDM supporters an opportunity to meet and interact with some of our amazing Miracle Kiddos and their Families. Come enjoy volleyball, yard games, cotton candy, snowcones, popcorn, and more. Entry is free for all registered Miracle Makers and $5 for all un-registered participants.

Miracle Family Events

Miracle Family Events are when we get to interact with our miracle families and kiddos, both the ones who are ambassadors for the hospital and ones who are being treated. These are events like hospital tours and craft nights with the kiddos!

Fundraising Events: Day of Miracles and May is for Miracles

SLUDM hosts two major fundraising events throughout the year, one in the spring and one in the fall though these events have similar layoyts their main purpose is fundraising and raising awareness. In the spring of 2019 we hosted our first ever May is for Miracles and raised just over $6,000 this was a great way to kick off our fundraising for the year! Day of Miracles occurs in November before our Main Event and last year we were able to raise over $49,000. Stay tuned for upcoming dates.
