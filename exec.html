2020 Executive Board and Contact Information

Executive Board

Four Teams

Advancement

  • This team handles everything related to Money! They focus on gaining corporate sponsorships, planning fundraising events and clever ways to help our members fundraise, finances, budgets, and profit shares. This team is made up of the Vice President of Advancement: Kaitlyn Vogel, Fundraising: McKenzie Walz, Finance Chair: Alexis Schillinger, Community Outreach Chair: Kaylee Gutzke, and Partnership Chair: Darren Manion. To get in contact with any of these people see below!

Marketing

  • This team handles any and all marketing of SLUDM. They are responsible for any and all graphic design elements, external communication, public relations and recruitment. This team is made up of the Vice President of Marketing: Grace Trello, Graphic Design Chair: Calyn Reardon, Recruitment Chair: Emily Schwab and Public Relations Chair: Ivana Peralta. For more information about any of these positions or contact information see below.

Membership

  • This team helps all differnt levels of participants, whether they be first time participants, veterans, or even alum. They help with internal communication and also work closely with our two local Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. This team is made up of the Vice President of Membership: Maddie Wempe, Family and Hospital Relations Chair: Maddie Arceo, Dancer Relations Chair: Emma Leoni, and Morale Chair: Gwen Cataldo. For more information about any of these positions or their contact information see below!

Operations

  • This team handles all things events. They are in charge of coming up with entertainment, and layouts for all of the events throughout the year. They also assist with all of our mini marathon programs. This team is made up of the Vice President of Operations: Megan Collins, Entertainment Chair: Megan Swanson, Event Management Chair: Claudia Sikora, High School Programming Chair: Kate Whitman and Special Events Chair: Olivia Pohlik. To learn more about these positions or to contact them please see information below.
