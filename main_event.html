Main Event

The Main Event

Our Main Event is a twelve hour long Celebration! During the event we celebrate a year’s worth of hard work, time, commitment, passion, dancing and fundraising done by those involved in our organization. We also celebrate the generosity of our donors and sponsors. Finally, we celebrate our Miracle Kiddos and their families, all of the birthdays, treatments, smiles, therapy sessions and obstacles they have overcome.

What to Expect

At the main event Miracle Makers will listen to Miracle stories from children treated at our two local Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals,as well as participate in an art auction with artwork made by our Miracle Kiddos. Miracle Makers will also be participating in Miracle Cup Games, learning the Morale Dance, eating great food, watching performances by our campus dance and acapella groups, and of course, DANCING! As the night comes to an end Miracle Makers will hear stories from some of their peers in the Chain of HOPE and will have their hospital bracelets removed in a ceremony called the Circle of Hope, after-which the fundraising total from the entire year will be revealed and everyone will join in the final Morale Dance performance!

Join Us in being One Generation Fighting for the Next by Registering for Our Main Event which will be held on November 23, 2019 in the Wool Ballrooms of the Busch Student Center from noon to midnight. Help us Make Miracles Happen For The Kids at St. Louis Children’s Miracle Network Hospital and SSM Health Cardinal Glennon Children’s Hospital.
