What is a Mini Marathon Program

Overview

Our Mini-Marathon Programs are an extension of Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon. These programs work closely with our High School Programming Chair to create programs at their schools. All of the funds they raise benefit SSM Health Cardinal Glennon and Saint Louis Childrenâ€™s Miracle Network Hospital. SLUDM works hard to provide each of our Mini-Marathons with the resources, support and guidance they need to create amazing events. These programs help to spread the mission of SLUDM as well as help to make more Miracles Happen For The Kids.

Francis Howell North
